Firm Profile

Meet the team at Chesapeake Accounting Group, PC. We’re proud to serve our community with integrity, professionalism, and personalized care.

Paige Biddlecomb

Paige, a partner at the firm, has been supporting clients in the community since 2001. She enjoys working closely with businesses and individuals to create tax strategies that truly fit their needs. She is a graduate of Virginia Commonwealth University with a Bachelor of Science in Business with a concentration in Accounting, and she earned her CPA license in 2007. Paige also serves on the Advisory Boards of Primis Bank and the Northumberland Family YMCA.

Paige is committed to helping clients make smart, informed decisions by combining her knowledge of their financial statements, unique situations, and forward-thinking tax planning.

Outside of work, she enjoys spending time with her family, going out on the boat, relaxing at the beach, and reading.

Tara Donahue

Tara joined the firm in 2016 and became a partner in 2021. She brings a strong foundation in business administration and management. She is a graduate of Old Dominion University, where she earned a Bachelor of Science in Business Administration with a concentration in Accounting and a minor in Business Management. Tara is currently in the process of obtaining her CPA license.

In addition to her work with the firm, Tara serves her community through appointments on the Town of Kilmarnock Planning Commission and the Board of Zoning Appeals. Her commitment to both professional growth and civic engagement reflects her dedication to providing informed, well-rounded service to clients.
